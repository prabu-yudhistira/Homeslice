01   Our Story

The comfort
of home,
without the
cooking.

Homeslice was born from a simple belief: that homemade comfort food, made with honest ingredients and genuine craft, should feel as warm as anything cooked at home.

Every dish on our menu is built from memory — the kind of food that quiets a room and starts a conversation at the same time. We don't chase trends. We chase flavour.

longer.

Homeslice was never only about the plate. It's the low golden light, the worn timber, the easy hum of a room that feels like someone's home — because that is exactly what we set out to build.

Come for an early lunch and let the afternoon unfold. Pull two tables together for the people you love. This is your corner of Solo to slow down in.
